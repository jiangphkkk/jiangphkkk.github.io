Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to deliver and enhance the PDF scanning, conversion, editing, and management features of the Service. The categories of data we may obtain include:

•                      Account Information: When you sign up or log in using Google, Apple ID, or other supported platforms, we receive your email address, full name, and profile picture (if provided). This data is used to create and manage your account, verify your identity, and sync documents and settings across devices.

•                      Advertising ID (IDFA / GAID): We collect your device’s advertising identifier to prevent fraudulent activity, maintain ad quality, and support personalized in-app offers and services.

•                      IP Address: Your IP address is collected to assess general region, enhance service security, prevent abuse, and comply with legal obligations.

•                      Device Information: Including device model, operating system version, screen resolution, system language, time zone, battery status, network type (Wi‑Fi / cellular), storage availability, and session timestamps.

•                      Document & Usage Data: We log scanning activity, file conversion tasks, document editing history, export/sharing actions, feature usage, session length, and preference settings. This helps us optimize speed, accuracy, and user experience.

•                      Crash & Performance Logs: When the app experiences errors, crashes, or performance issues, we collect diagnostic logs to identify bugs, improve stability, and fix technical problems.

•                      Installed Apps (with consent): Our integrated ad SDK may request access to your installed apps list on first launch to improve ad relevance. You may decline this permission without losing access to core scanning features. We do not store or process this data.

Account & Data Deletion

You may request permanent deletion of your account and all associated Personal Information at any time:

Please include your registered email for verification. We will process and confirm deletion within 14 business days. Deleted documents, preferences, and account data cannot be recovered.
